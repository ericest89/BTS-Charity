About Us

At Bigger Than Sports, we believe in the transformative power of community and the boundless potential of our youth.

Located in the vibrant heart of South Seattle, we are more than just a nonprofit organization — we are a movement dedicated to creating positive change, one child, one family, and one neighborhood at a time. Our mission is simple yet profound: to bring the youth of South Seattle closer together, empowering them to lead fulfilling lives and pursue their dreams. We recognize that many young people face formidable challenges, from the allure of violence and drugs to the pervasive sense of isolation in our modern world. Through our programs and initiatives, we strive to provide a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment where every child can thrive.
